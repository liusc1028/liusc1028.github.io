Dr. Sicong Liu (劉思聰)

Address: Haiyun Campus, Xiamen University, Xiamen 361005, China P.R.

E-mail: liusc at xmu dot edu dot cn/ liusc1028 at gmail dot com



Sicong Liu received his B.S.E. in electronic engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China in 2012, and the PhD degree, supervised by Prof. Jian Song (IEEE Fellow, ITU 6A Group Deputy Leader), in electronic engineering in Tsinghua University in 2017 with the highest honor. From 2010 to 2011, he was a visiting scholar in Dept. Electronic and Communications Engineering, City University of Hong Kong. He served as a senior research engineer in Research Dept. Network Technologies, Huawei Technologies Co. Ltd, from 2017 to 2018. Currently, he is an assistant professor in School of Informatics, Xiamen University.

Sicong Liu has published over 40 journal and conference research papers. He owns 8 Chinese invention patents. He is one of the core members that draft the Broadband Power Line Communications Standard in China. He has won the National Graduate Fellowship Award twice in 2014 and 2016, the Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award of Tsinghua University, the Outstanding PhD Graduate Award of Tsinghua University, and the Outstanding Graduate Award of Beijing City. He is a reviewer of IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, IEEE Transactions on Communications, IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid, IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, IEEE Transactions on Cybernetics, and IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications. He is on the Editorial Board of the Future Internet Journal SI on Massive MIMO. He served as a the Technical Program Committee member/publication chair in many international academic conferences, including IEEE ICC, IEEE Globecom, IEEE SmartGridComm, ITNAC, DICC. He is a member of IEEE.

His research interests mainly lie in sparse signal processing, interference suppression, wireless communications, physical-layer and network security, massive MIMO, power line and visible light communications, machine learning, and emerging 5G technologies.
